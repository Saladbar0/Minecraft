Minecraft is an open world survival game that came out in 2009, so it's been around for a very long time. This is a website that shows the statistics and some interesting information about some of the mobs in Minecraft, from passive mobs like the Axolotl or the Sheep, to boss mobs like the Ender Dragon.
Pretty soon, December to be exact, the Minecraft 1.18 update will be coming out, Caves & Cliffs Part II, and recently, the new 1.19 Wild update was announced!
Some of the new content in the 1.18 Caves & Cliffs Part II will be an increased world height, a Cave and Mountain overhaul, the new Copper Ore, Axolotls, Lush Caves, and more!
In the 1.19 Wild Update, There will be a Swamp Overhaul, a new wood type, the Deep Dark, Frogs, and more!
Stay tuned to find more information about the new updates and new fun facts!

Fun Fact: The new Warden in the 1.19 Wild Update will be one of the most dangerous mobs in all of Minecraft, but it can only be found in the Deep Dark Cities.
