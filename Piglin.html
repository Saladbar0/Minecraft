Piglin

The piglin is a unique mob that generates in pretty much every nether biome.

  • Trivia
      • The Piglin will only attack the player if the player is not wearing any gold armor, or if the player breaks any form of gold or chests near the piglin. The piglin can be distracted if the player throws a gold ingot or any gold item at it, to which it will either trade the gold ingot for another item, or it will equip the armor or weapon that is thrown.
