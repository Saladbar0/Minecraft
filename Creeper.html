Creeper

The Creeper was actually introduced to the game by accident when the developers were trying to add a pig in the game.

  • Gunpowder
      • If a player approaches a creeper, it will explode shortly after. The explosion will do a lot of damage to the player and make a massive hole in the ground.
      If the player manages to kill the creeper, then it will by chance drop gunpowder, which can be used to make firework rockets or TNT. TNT obviously explodes, and fireowrk rockets
      Can either be used as decoration or to propel the player when using an Elytra.
