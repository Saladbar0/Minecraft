Enderman

The Enderman is a unique mob because it has the ability to teleport. It only gets attacks the player if either the player hits it first or the player looks into the enderman's eyes. The enderman also has the ability to pick up certain blocks and move them elsewhere.

  • Honey Bottles/Honey Combs/Trivia
      • The enderman is afraid of the Endermite mob and will try to attack it. The enderman is a very strong mob with 40 hp, while the player only has 20hp.
