Mooshroom

The Mooshroom is a passive mob that spawns uniquely in the mushroom fields or mushroom idland biomes.

  • Mooshroom
      • Simalarly to the cow, if the player interacts with the mooshroom with a bowl in their hand, the bowl will be filled up with mushroom stew instead of milk. If the player interacts with the rarer, brown mooshroom in the same way, then the bowl will be filled with Suspicious Stew that can give various status effects.
