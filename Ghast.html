Ghast

The ghast is an enemy found in the Nether that flies around and shoots fireballs at the player if it gets near.

  • Ghast Tears/Trivia
      • The Ghast will shoot fireballs that deal similar damage to the Creeper explosion. The Sounds for the ghast were actually made by a cat.
      If the player kills a ghast then it will drop gunpowder, and occasionally Ghast Tears, which can be used to craft End Crystals which explode on contact.
