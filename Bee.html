Bee

The bee was added in the 1.15 update which was a smaller update, but a good one nonetheless. It added the Honey Bottles and Honey Comb which are both pretty important items.

  • Honey Bottles/Honey Combs/Trivia
      • If the player hits a bee, then the bee will sting the player, inflicting it with poison. Similarly to real life, after the bee stings the player, the bee dies.
      Honey bottles can be used to craft honey blocks, and honey blocks can be used for many different machines. Honeycombs are more for decoration than anything else.
