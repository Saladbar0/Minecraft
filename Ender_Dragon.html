Ender Dragon

The Ender Dragon is conisidered the Final Boss of Minecraft. There is only one naturally spawning Dragon, but it can be resummoned by the player using 4 end crystals. The Dragon is found in the End Dimansion. Once it is defeated, the player gains access to two main items:

  • The Dragon Egg:
      • The Dragon Egg is a trophy more than anything. It doesn't have a practical use besides sitting as a reminder that you have defeated the Ender Dragon.
  • Elytra:
      • Elytra are one of the most useful items in all of Minecraft. It unlocks the ability of flight (well it's actually gliding but if you pair it with Fireworks then it's flying).
