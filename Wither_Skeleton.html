Wither Skeleton

The Wither Skeleton is a deadly mob found only in Nether Fortresses.

  • Trivia/Wither Skeleton Skulls
      • The Wither Skeleton is a deadly mob standing at 3 blocks tall, wielding a stone sword, and inflicting the Wither debuff to the player when the skeleton makes contact, but there are some upsides to fighting it. If the player is very lucky, on a 1/100 chance, a Wither Skeleton can drop it's head (this chance can be raised with the Looting enchantment of any tier). If the player gets three Wither Skeleton Skulls, then the player can summon the Wither. One of the bosses of Minecraft.
