Strider

The strider was added in the 1.16 nether update. It is a ridable mob that acts as a boat that can ride on lava, so it is a very useful mob.

  • Strider
      • If the player puts a saddle on the strider and holds a warped fungus on a stick, the strider will move in the direction that the player is looking in. When the strider is outside of lava or not standing on magma, it will get very cold and move very slowly. The striders are also very sad :(
