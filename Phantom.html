Phantom

The phantom only appears after the player hasn't slept for 3 consecutive nights. The more nights the player hasn't slept, the more phantoms appear, with a cap of 7.

  • Phantom Membranes/Trivia
      • The phantom was added in the 1.13 update after it was voted into the game by the community. There is an achievement for killing 2 phantoms with one arrow
      from a piercing crossbow. The phantom membranes that it drops can be used to repair the players elytra if the player does not already have mending, in which case it can be
      repaired using EXP.
