Iron Golem

The Iron Golem is found naturally generating in Villages. They are also able to be summoned by the player by placing 4 iron blocks and a carved pumpkin in a cross.

  • Trivia
      • The Iron Golem is the defender of the village, standing 3 blocks tall and 3 blocks wide, capable of dealing up to 16 damage in one hit, they are a very tough mob. They will only attack the player if they are damaged by the player or if any of the villagers are damaged near the iron golem by the player.
