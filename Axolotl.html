Axolotl

The axolotl was introduced in the 1.17 caves & cliffs update. Their only practical use is fighting guardians in ocean monuments with you.

  • Cool axolotls
      • You can caputure an axolotl in a bucket and make it your friend. If released from the bucket near an ocean monument, they will fight the guardians alongside you.
      If they get low on health, they will go into a "play dead" state where they sit still and regenerate health. Enemies will usually not attack the axolotls in this stage.
