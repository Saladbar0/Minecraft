Goat

The goat is a new animal that was added in the 1.17 Caves & Cliffs update. It only attacks the player if they are provoked first.

  • Trivia
      • There is an achievement for getting in a boat with a goat called Whatever floats your goat. There is also a rare variant of the goat called the screaming goat, which.. well... screams.
