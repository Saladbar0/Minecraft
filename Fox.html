Fox

The Fox is a passive mob that spawns in the Spruce Forests and Tundra Biomes.

  • Foxes/Trivia
      • The fox is pretty unique because it can pick up most items that the player drops on the ground; if the items are consumables, then the fox can eat them and get the buffs from them. If it is an enchanted item, then when it attacks a chicken that enchantment is used.
