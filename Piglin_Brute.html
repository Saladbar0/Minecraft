Piglin Brute

The Piglin Brute is a variant of Piglin that is far deadlier than the normal piglin. It is actually commonly considered the most deadly regular mob in all of Minecraft.

  • Trivia
      • Only generating in the Bastion Remnants found in the nether, the Piglin Brute cannot be disctracted in any way and will always attempt to attack the player. With it's golden axe, it can sometimes deal upwards of 9 hearts of damage and even kill a endgame level player if the situation is not handled properly.
