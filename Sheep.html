Sheep

Ah the sheep. One of the most bland mobs of minecraft. It can be found in most biomes.

  • Sheep
      • The sheep can be sheared with shears to acquire wool. If the player dyes the sheep a different color, then you will get the according color of wool when you shear it. If the player names the sheep Jeb_, the sheep's wool will turn rainbow, but when sheared the sheep will drop the color of wool it was before being named.
